Our story

Your Mobile Mechanic in Dallas – Committed to Quality & Convenience

To provide Dallas drivers with honest, high-quality auto repair and maintenance wherever they are, eliminating the hassle of traditional shops.

Mobile Auto Repair & Maintenance was founded by a team of ASE-certified mechanics who saw a need for convenient, transparent car care in Dallas. Tired of long wait times and inflated shop prices, they built a mobile service that brings the workshop to the customer. Starting with a single van, they have grown into a trusted name serving hundreds of Dallas residents and businesses.

Today, we continue to prioritize quality, integrity, and customer satisfaction. Every mechanic is rigorously trained and equipped with the latest tools. We believe in educating our customers and providing fair pricing. Whether you're at home, work, or stuck on the roadside, we're just a call away.
