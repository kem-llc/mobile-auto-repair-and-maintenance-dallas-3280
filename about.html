Our story

Your Trusted Mobile Mechanic in Dallas

To provide convenient, high-quality auto repair that fits your schedule and budget – without the hassle of a traditional shop.

Founded by a team of ASE-certified mechanics, Mobile Auto Repair & Maintenance was born from a simple idea: why should car repair be inconvenient? We saw that busy Dallas residents needed a better option – one that saves time and reduces stress. So we packed our tools and brought the shop to you.

Since then, we've helped thousands of drivers get back on the road quickly and safely. Our commitment to transparency, quality parts, and expert workmanship has made us a trusted name in mobile auto repair across Dallas. We're proud to be your go-to mobile mechanic.
